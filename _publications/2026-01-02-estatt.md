EstAtt is a linear-complexity attention framework based on sign-driven estimation for long-context inference workloads.

The method uses LUT-based filtering to reduce attention complexity from quadratic to linear and pairs this algorithmic insight with an NDP-oriented VPE architecture. The design lowers communication overhead while preserving strong model quality on long-context tasks.

This work is currently under review at ICCAD.
